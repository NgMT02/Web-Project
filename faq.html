FAQs

Frequently Asked Questions

Do you offer free delivery, and how long will it take to receive my order ?

Yes, we provide free delivery with purchase above RM100. Your order will arrive within 5 working days.

What payment methods do you accept ?

We accept a variety of payment methods to ensure a convenient shopping experience for our customers. You can pay using credit cards (Visa, MasterCard), online banking, and other digital wallets such as Touch 'n Go and Boost.

Can I return my purchased product ?

In the rare event that our products fail to meet our commitment to quality, We will endeavour to resolve all requests for return and refund within 7 days from the date you received the product.

How should I store my spreads, and how long do they last ?

Our spreads should be stored in a cool, dry place. Once opened, they can be kept at room temperature for up to 3 months.

Are your spreads organic and non-GMO ?

Yes, all our spreads are made with organic and non-GMO ingredients. We source our nuts from certified organic farms that follow sustainable and eco-friendly farming practices. We believe in providing you with the healthiest and most natural product possible.
